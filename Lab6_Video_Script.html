"The most interesting thing I learned was the importance of proper scale selection. Using a sqrt scale instead of linear for dot sizes ensures the visual area is proportional to the data value, preventing perceptual distortion. Combined with D3's brush extent configuration and the raise method to fix layer ordering, these small technical details make the difference between a broken and a professional visualization."
